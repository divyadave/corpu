About CorpU

CorpU is a newly accredited university that hires a significant number of casual or sessional staff each semester to facilitate classes, labs, and workshops. The sessional staff is managed by permanent or long-term contract staff members who are responsible for finding, training, and scheduling qualified sessionals for the units or subjects they oversee. Depending on availability and expertise, a sessional staff member can be engaged by one or multiple units, with up to 20 unique sessionals involved in each unit, depending on student enrollment.
